Jointly developed with my team at **Project MANAS**, this is the complete software stack for our autonomous ground vehicle that competed at IGVC 2025 in the USA, securing 3rd place globally.

Includes:
- Real-time ODLC (Object Detection, Localization & Classification)
- Probabilistic occupancy grid mapping
- End-to-end autonomous navigation
